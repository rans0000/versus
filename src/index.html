About Us

Versus is an integrated maintenance service provider. We have built strong relationship across Qatar and the company commenced operations with a carefully selected team of experts in order to hit the ground running.

Our highly experienced technicians and maintenance management team works with clients to ensure safe, compliant and professional built environments through evaluation, maintenance, repair and upgrade of their facilities.

We have strategy to hiring expert technicians with specialist knowledge, giving them continuously training and improving skills. We allocate the right people in the right place at the right time to bring the best outcomes. This improves and standardizes service delivery across a portfolio and creates significant cost savings for our clients.
