About us

Versus is an integrated service provider for maintenance and energy efficient solutions which reduce cost of utilities like water, electricity and HVAC for residential and commercial buildings. We achieve energy efficiency by using innovative technologies and best practices. We have built strong relationship across Qatar and the company commenced operations with a carefully selected team of experts in order to hit the ground running.

Our highly experienced team ensure clients to provide solutions and maintenance of the facilities. We have strategy to hiring expert technicians with specialist knowledge, giving them continuously training and improving skills. We allocate the right people in the right place at the right time to bring the best outcomes. This improves and standardizes service delivery across a portfolio and creates significant cost savings for our clients.
